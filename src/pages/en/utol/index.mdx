## What is UTOL (UTokyo LMS)
{:#about}
UTOL is the learning management system of the University of Tokyo. Academic and administrative staff can post announcements or course materials for each course individually. In addition, students are able to submit assignments or take quizzes.

UTokyo Account is required to use the UTOL. If you have not yet used the [UTokyo Account](/utokyo_account/), please first check “[Getting Ready for ICT systems at UTokyo (for New Students)](/oc/)”, “[Getting Ready for ICT systems at the UTokyo (for Faculty Members)](/faculty_members/)”.

### Settings to receive information notices

You can receive information notices via email, LINE, or UTokyo Slack whenever there are updates on courses you are in charge of or are registered for. Please refer to “[Settings to receive information notices with the UTOL](notification/)” for the setup procedure.
